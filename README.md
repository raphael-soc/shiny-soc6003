Shiny-SOC6003
This open-source Shiny app was written in R-Studio is designed to help students practice and improve their statistical skills. It’s completely free to use and intended for non-commercial learning purposes only.

The app’s functionality is simple and includes a range of self-explanatory tasks, such as multiple-choice questions, short calculations, and interpretations of statistical outputs.

If you use or reference this app in your work, please consider crediting the original creators.
